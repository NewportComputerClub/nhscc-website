KnightHacks is a hackathon hosted by our club, and takes place February. Middle and high school students are welcome to join us for 6 hours of pure hacking. There will be judges and prizes for the top hacks!

Competitions

PSCSTA & CodeMIHS

Computer club members participate in programming competitions such as PSCSTA (a TEALS and Microsoft hosted event) and CodeMIHS (hosted at Mercer Island High School). Both of these competitions test advanced algorithmic concepts, and have various prizes!

build

DefHacks & CodeDay

DefHacks (sponsored and hosted by Microsoft) and CodeDay (hosted by StudentRND) are hackathons that place emphasis on creating new products and (somewhat) practical applications and software. Apply your coding knowledge to build something new!

USACO

The United States Computing Olympiad is an competitive, nationwide algorithmic programming competition, done online. Students around the country compete to represent the United States in the Informatics Olympiad by solving extremely difficult problems.
